Runbook: Human-Centric Automation

Built by Rodney Brooks, Runbook is a pragmatic, low-code platform that empowers admins, analysts, and engineers to design and trigger complex automation without writing brittle scripts or relying on cloud lock-in.

Run SQL. Trigger PowerShell. Orchestrate C# jobs. All in one place. All under your control.
